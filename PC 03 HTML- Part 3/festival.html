FESTIVALS

1. DUSSEHRA:

Dussehra, also known as Vijayadashami, is a major Hindu festival celebrated at the end of Navaratri every year. It signifies the victory of good over evil. The festival commemorates the victory of Lord Rama over the demon king Ravana. Huge effigies of Ravana, Meghnath, and Kumbhkaran are burned to celebrate the triumph of good forces. People enact scenes from the Ramayana, known as Ramleela, culminating in the burning of the effigies. Dussehra is also celebrated as a reminder to remove negativity and evil thoughts from our lives.
